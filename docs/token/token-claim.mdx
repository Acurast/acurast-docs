The [`pallet-acurast-token-claim`](https://github.com/Acurast/acurast-substrate/tree/main/pallets/token-claim) pallet is used to distributed ACU tokens awarded to users via Air Drop campaigns and loot box rewards. The tokens are vested over a period of time that depends on the specific campaign.

## How Vesting Works

Vested tokens are not transferred to the recipient all at once. Instead, they unlock gradually over a fixed duration measured in blocks. The tokens remain on the funder account until explicitly claimed by the recipient. 
At any point after the vesting period has started, the recipient can trigger a transfer of however many tokens have unlocked since their last claim.

This means claiming is an ongoing process, each time a recipient calls claim, they receive only the portion that has vested since the last time they did so.

## Claim Types

Token distributions are organized into **claim types**. Each claim type represents a distinct distribution campaign and is associated with:

- A **funder account** — the account from which tokens are transferred as they vest.
- A **vesting duration** — the number of blocks over which the full allocation unlocks.

A recipient may be eligible for multiple claim types. Each claim type is independent: its own vesting schedule starts when the recipient registers their claim on-chain.

## The Claim Flow

Claiming vested tokens happens in two stages:

### 1. Registering a Claim

To register a claim, the recipient submits a cryptographic proof on-chain. This proof was issued off-chain by the funder account to claim a specific amount of tokens under that specific claim type.

Once the proof is accepted:
- A vesting schedule is created on-chain for the recipient.
- The starting block is recorded, and tokens begin unlocking from that point forward.
- The claim cannot be registered again — it is one-time per claim type per recipient.

### 2. Vesting (Unlocking Tokens)

After registering a claim, the recipient can trigger a vest at any time. Each vest call calculates how many tokens have unlocked since the last vest and transfers that amount from the funder to the recipient's account.

This can be done as often or as infrequently as the recipient chooses. Once all tokens have been transferred, the vesting schedule is removed.
